Jonathan Huang

Who am I?

Hello! I'm Jonathan Huang. I was born and raised in Mountain View, CA.

I love traveling and exploring. I'm not much of a historical sightseeing person, but I love going to new places and trying new things. I've been to over half of the U.S. so far and hopefully will visit them all one day.

I don't eat beef. Yes, I'll buy chicken or fish at a steakhouse and remove pepperoni from pizza if necessary. It's not a religious reason like what most people may think...but it's a story I can tell someday!

I attended UC San Diego for my undergraduate career and graduated with a degree in Human Biology. Initially, my career goal was to become an optometrist. Near the end of my journey at UCSD, I developed an interest for coding and computer science.

Then and Now

During and after college, I worked as an optometric assistant. As a result, I know a lot about the eye and vision correction! Because of my experience with it, I decided to work in a familiar field after graduating as I took classes on the side to explore my interest in programming.

I'm currently a Phase 0 student enrolled in Dev Bootcamp. I'll be attending Phase 1-3 in San Francisco and hope to reach my goal of becoming a great web developer!
